Features

Beautiful Bookmarking

Visual grid & list views, website previews & icons, dark & light themes, fast search and filtering.

Organize Your Way

Collections & folders, tags & smart filtering, favorites & quick access.

Built on Open Source

Based on GPL‑licensed SiteBar concepts, fully GPL‑compatible codebase, transparent development & roadmap.

Self‑Hosted First

Run it on your own server, no SaaS lock‑in, ideal for homelabs, teams, and communities.

API‑Ready Architecture

Clean REST API, automation & integrations, designed for browser sync.

Rooted in History, Built for the Future

Before cloud services and closed platforms, SiteBar proved that bookmarks could live on your server — open, flexible, and community‑maintained. NetSurfer Bookmarks continues that vision: respecting the GPL license, preserving the original philosophy, modernizing the architecture, UI, and performance. This is a continuation, not a rewrite of history.

Open Source & Licensing

NetSurfer Bookmarks is fully open‑source under the GNU General Public License (GPL), compatible with the original SiteBar license. Contributions must remain open, ensuring long‑term freedom for users and contributors.
